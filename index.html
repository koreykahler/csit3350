1. Shrimp Fettuccine Alfredo

Shrimp Fettuccine Alfredo is a hearty meal, featuring creamy cheeses and tasty noodles, topped with delicious gamey shrimp. Serves 3-4 people and cooks in about 15 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 8oz cream cheese
  • 1lb uncooked peeled shrimp
  • 1/3 cup milk (more milk for thinner sauce)
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 tblsp butter, 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Pasta noodles of choice (e.g. approximately 9oz of fettuccine noodles)

Directions:

  1. Grate the parmesan cheese and peel the shrimp.
  2. Begin preparing the pasta by bringing 4 cups of water to a rapid boil.
  3. Turn down to medium heat and add the noodles, then let them simmer for around 10 minutes.
  4. While the noodles are cooking, begin preparing the alfredo sauce by adding butter, garlic, and shrimp to a large frying pan.
  5. On medium heat, saute the ingredients until the shrimp is cooked.
  6. Once the shrimp is cooked, begin breaking the cream cheese into chunks and add it into the mix. Stir until creamy.
  7. Once the cream cheese turns into a saucey consistency, add milk and stir until a light simmer.
  8. Add parmesan cheese and continue stirring until the cheese is mixed completely.
  9. Remove from heat. Strain the noodles, place them on a plate and pour the alfredo sauce on top.
  10. Your meal is ready to be served!

2. Perfect Potato Salad

This dish includes a healthy mix of tasty veggies like horseradish and celery, along with a flavorful sauce. Serves 8 people and can be prepared in about 20 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 5 Potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-size chunks (about 2 lbs)
  • 1 cup Plain Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tbsp Prepared Horseradish
  • 4 Radishes, thinly sliced
  • 2 stalks Celery, diced
  • 2 large Green Onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Dill, chopped

Directions:

  1. Place potatoes in a pot and cover with water.
  2. Begin cooking the potatoes. Partially lid and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until potatoes are tender (5 mins). Donâ€™t overcook. Drain well.
  3. Meanwhile in a large bowl, stir yogurt with mayonnaise, horseradish, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir in radishes, celery, green onions, parsley, and dill until mixed.
  5. Add warm potatoes and stir to combine. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving. Taste and adjust seasonings if desired.

3. Easy Chicken and Rice Casserole

Super simple and delicious chicken casserole. Serves 4 people and takes about 45 minutes to cook.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup celery, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter or margarine
  • 2 cups chicken, cooked and chopped
  • 1 3/4 cups hot water
  • 2/3 cup long grain white rice
  • 1 can (10 oz) cream of mushroom
  • 1 cup peas and carrots
  • 1 tsp dried thyme and rosemary

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a large frying pan, cook onion and celery in butter until soft.
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  4. Bake in covered 8 cup baking dish in the oven for about 30 minutes or until rice is cooked.
